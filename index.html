Our Heritage

Founded in 1930, J.B. College has been a cornerstone of higher education in Assam for nearly a century. In 2023, it was elevated to a State University — Jagannath Barooah University — reflecting its growth and academic excellence.

About Us

A Century of Academic Excellence

Jagannath Barooah University (formerly J.B. College) is located in the cultural hub of Jorhat, Assam. The institution has maintained a tradition of academic rigor, producing graduates who have gone on to lead in every field — from science and commerce to arts and public service.

Offering over 30 undergraduate and postgraduate programs across Arts, Science, and Commerce streams, JBU combines a rich legacy with modern infrastructure. The sprawling campus features state-of-the-art labs, a large central library, an indoor stadium, and a 3,000-capacity auditorium.

How to Apply

Admission Process

  • 1

    Visit SAMARTH Portal

    All admissions are conducted through the Assam SAMARTH e-governance portal. Register with your valid email and phone number.

  • 2

    Fill Application Form

    Complete the online application form with accurate academic details, preferred program, and upload necessary documents.

  • 3

    Merit / Selection Test

    Most programs follow merit-based selection. Certain subjects require a separate selection test. Check the specific program criteria.

  • 4

    Confirmation & Enrollment

    Upon selection, complete fee payment and document verification. Attend orientation and commence your academic journey.
